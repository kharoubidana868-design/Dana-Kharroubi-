Approved 2027 Code and International Standards

The 2027 World Anti-Doping Code (Code) and International Standards (Standards) were approved on 5 December by the Agency’s Foundation Board and Executive Committee respectively, at WADA’s sixth World Conference on Doping in Sport in Busan, Republic of Korea. The 2027 Code and Standards, accessible through the links below, will come into force on 1 January 2027.
