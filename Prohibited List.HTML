Prohibited List

The World Anti-Doping Agency (WADA) has published the List of Prohibited Substances and Methods (List). The list comes into effect on 01 January of each year.

Under the FIG Anti-Doping Rules, it is each athletes or other persons responsibility for knowing what constitutes an anti-doping rule violation and the substances and methods which have been included on the Prohibited List.

Therefore, it is essential that all athletes or other persons review carefully the Prohibited List, particularly in cases where they intend to use supplements or medication.
